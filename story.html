Our Story

Welcome to Christie and Chris's Cooking Cabin

Founded in 2007, Christie and Chris's Cooking Cabin has been empowering college students with the art of cooking, blending culinary skills with academic education to foster independence, creativity, and connection.

  • 2007: Launch of our first cooking class designed for college students.
  • 2010: Expansion of our curriculum to include international cuisine.
  • 2015: Introduction of advanced culinary courses and workshops.
  • 2020: Online classes introduced to reach students nationwide.
