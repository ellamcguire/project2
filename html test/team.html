Meet Our Management Team

Passionate individuals making culinary arts accessible to college students

Ella McGuire - Co-Founder & CEO

Background:

Ella, a Purdue alumna with a degree in Hospitality and Tourism Management, founded BoilerBites with a vision to empower students through cooking. Her passion for culinary arts and education drives the academy's mission to innovate and inspire.

Sam Wadlington - Culinary Director

Background:

A culinary prodigy since his teenage years, Alex transformed his passion into a career. After graduating from a prestigious culinary school, he joined BoilerBites to share his extensive knowledge and skills, specializing in sustainable cooking practices.

Isabella Pearson - Operations Manager

Background:

With a knack for organization and a degree in Business Administration from Purdue, Isabella ensures the smooth operation of our classes and events. Her innovative strategies have significantly enhanced the student experience at BoilerBites.

Hey Cheuk Lo - Marketing Coordinator

Background:

Hey Cheuk, a current senior at Purdue studying Marketing, joined the team to help spread the word about BoilerBites. His creative campaigns have successfully increased student engagement and enrollment.
