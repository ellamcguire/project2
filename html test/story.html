Welcome to BoilerBites Culinary Academy

Empowering college students with the art of cooking since 2007

Our Story

Founded in 2007, BoilerBites Culinary Academy emerged from a simple, yet powerful desire: to teach college students the indispensable skill of cooking. Recognizing that many students entering college lacked the fundamental knowledge to prepare healthy, tasty meals for themselves, BoilerBites was born.

Our Mission

At the heart of BoilerBites is a mission to empower students. We believe cooking is not just about nutrition; it's about independence, creativity, and connection. Our classes are designed not only to teach cooking techniques but also to instill confidence and foster a community of food enthusiasts.

A Brief History

What started in a modest kitchen space on the outskirts of the Purdue University campus has grown into a thriving community. In our early days, we focused on basic cooking techniques and easy-to-prepare recipes. Over the years, we've expanded our curriculum to include a wide range of cuisines, advanced culinary skills, and even courses on food sustainability and ethics.

Throughout our journey, we've been guided by feedback from our students, evolving to meet their changing needs and interests. Today, BoilerBites Culinary Academy stands as a testament to the power of community and the joy of cooking. We continue to welcome new students each semester, eager to embark on their culinary adventures with us.
